Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our counselling services, process, and policies.

What can I expect in my first session?

Your first session is all about getting to know each other and understanding your goals. We’ll explore what brought you here, what you’d like to achieve, and ensure you feel comfortable moving forward. It’s a space built on trust and openness.

How long does a typical counselling session last?

Most sessions last around 50 minutes. This provides enough time to explore your thoughts and emotions without feeling rushed. Longer sessions can be arranged if needed.

Is what I share confidential?

Yes. Everything shared during counselling is kept confidential, except in rare cases where there is a serious risk of harm or legal obligation to disclose. We’ll always explain the limits of confidentiality at the start of our work together.

What are your fees and do you accept insurance?

Fees depend on the service provided. Check the booking page for details. While we don’t handle insurance directly, you may request an invoice to submit for reimbursement if your provider allows it.

How many sessions will I need?

Everyone’s journey is unique. Some find what they need in just a few sessions, while others prefer ongoing support. We’ll tailor the plan to your goals and comfort level.
